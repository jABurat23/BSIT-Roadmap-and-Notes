# 📖 Study Tips & How I Stay Organized

> This is how I personally manage my time, notes, and sanity throughout my BSIT degree.
> Take what works for you, ignore what doesn't.

---

## 🧠 My Personal Study Philosophy

I do things **slowly and deliberately** — rushing leads to stress, and stress leads to nothing getting done properly. The goal is steady progress, not a sprint.

---

## 🗂️ How I Tackle My Tasks

1. **Categorize first** — I sort everything by how well I understand it, from hardest to easiest
2. **Hard things go first** — I deal with the difficult stuff while my energy is fresh, so it's not hanging over my head later
3. **Important things never get skipped** — if something is critical, it gets done no matter what
4. **Easy things last** — by then it's almost relaxing 😄

---

## 📝 Note-Taking Habits

- One folder per subject, one file per topic
- I write notes in Markdown so they render nicely on GitHub
- I commit notes after every study session
- If I don't understand something, I mark it with `❓` to revisit later
